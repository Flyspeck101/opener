DkDPC

By Flyspeck101

DkDPC is a Double Skim opener that has a high 8-line Perfect Clear chance. It was independently discovered by me in August 2023. The original opener is 777's Mechanical TSD.

Timing

This opener is most useful against PCO, which is incredibly common for early T. When used against PCO or a similar no attack first bag opener, the goal is to complete the setup as fast as possible, to ensure the single garbage line is sent in time.

Against TSD openers, you should tank the TSD in order to receive a full four lines of garbage. Then, you should complete the second bag TSD as fast as possible to minimise your chances of receiving the next TSD before the perfect clear, as the back-to-back bonus will send one line, preventing you from PCing. Then on the third bag, you should try to find a save I solution to Colour Clear, so you can use the held I piece to clear the 4 lines of garbage, performing a Perfect Clear that sends you back to a fresh bag.

The most difficult opener to use this against is a TSS opener. As with TSD openers you should tank TSS, to send one garbage line and prevent any future PC. After that, try to go for TSD C/D, then freestyle.
