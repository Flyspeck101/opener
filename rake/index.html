Rake Stacking

By Flyspeck101

Rake Stacking is a double TST opener. It was made by me in early July 2023. This document assumes Jstris/TETR.IO 180s.

Bag 1

In bag 1, the O is usually held until the S has been placed. There are two ways to build the first bag, with JTI being interchangeable. The cover for the first bag is 60.87% with mirror.

Bag 2

The second bag is 100%. Some difficult kicks may be needed to place the L piece:

Bag 3

The third bag is 100%.

Bag 4+

After the third bag there are multiple routes you can take.

14L PC

The residue has a 100% chance of a Perfect Clear, with only 6 minimals. Note that T is fixed.

TSM Kaidan

You can always stack this TSM. The residue is very good and you can easilly do a Kaidan.

Dulse Flame

This setup is not 100%. If S and Z have been placed the L cannot be spun in. After completing the TSD you can always follow up with a TD.
