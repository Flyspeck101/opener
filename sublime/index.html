Sublime Stacking

By Flyspeck101

Sublime Stacking is a TST opener, made in August 2023 by me. It is called Sublime Stacking for literally no particular reason. This document assumes 180 spins.

Bag 1

The first bag needs an early T and J (T and L for the mirrored version). With mirror, it has a 75.55% build rate. The S (or Z) is held until the second bag.

Bag 2

The second bag is 100%. Where the held S is placed depends on the order of S and Z in the second bag.
